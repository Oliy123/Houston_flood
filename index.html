At Flood-Risk Population in Houston

Census tracts that may be vulnerable during a flood



Hover over a map for more info

This web map shows flood susceptibility area and demographic data by census tract for Houston, Texas. The darker yellow has a higher rate of the area flood rescue. And also, by hovering over the map, we can get the percentage of households without a vehicle. This app helps answer the question: Where should we provide evacuation assistance during a flood rescue in Houston? Use the Legend tools to learn more about the data shown on the map. Hover over census tracts to see pop-up information, including the percentage of households without a vehicle.
